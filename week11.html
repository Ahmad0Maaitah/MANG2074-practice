🔬 Research Methods Lab — Research Sprint

The final week puts the whole module together: a real piece of empirical research is a pipeline — question → data → model class → diagnostics → interpretation — and the hard part is not running regressions, it is not fooling yourself. Below: concept cards for the methodology that examiners (and referees) actually test, four interactive platforms (build a mini paper, manufacture false discoveries by p-hacking, stress-test a "finding" for robustness, and translate statistics into economics), a clinic of research pathologies, the Research Sprint game, and eight code challenges spanning the whole module.
