AR, MA and ARMA processes each leave a distinctive fingerprint in the ACF and PACF — and reading those fingerprints is how econometricians choose a model. This week you drive the ARMA machine, learn the identification rules, then test yourself by unmasking hidden processes from their correlograms alone.
