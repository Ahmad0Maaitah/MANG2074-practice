⚖️ Panel Data — Hausman Court

Panel data follow the same firms over time: yit = αi + βxit + εit. The firm effect αi captures everything fixed about firm i — management quality, industry, culture. If αi is correlated with x, pooled OLS (and random effects) are biased; fixed effects (the "within" estimator) survives by using only variation inside each firm. Below you can watch pooled OLS lie in real time, then take the judge's seat in Hausman Court.
